Poetry Paradise

Poetry Paradise is a web application that not only provides access to master pieces of art from talented people around the globe, but also allows users to post their own compositions, receive feedback from others, edit someone else’s work, and translate poems into other languages (to expand their audience).

SearchSmarter

SearchSmarter is a Chrome extension that can optimize users' Google search results. Users can then choose varies ways to optimize their search, such as searching only for scholarly articles or a particular file type, such as PowerPoints or PDFs. SearchSmarter is automatically enabled only when users are on the Google search page. Despite this, SearchSmarter doesn't have an obnoxious popup that detracts from users' googling experience. Instead, the extension, if hovered over, simply asks: "Are you unhappy with your search results?" Then users can start optimizing through the links and suggestions the extension provides.

Music Editor

Music Editor is a Java project that can visually display and play music. Users can choose between a visual view, which represents a music sheet with notes corresponding to the song that they want to have played, or a composite view, which displays the notes and plays the music. Additionally, for the duration of the song, the composite view had a re-rendered red line indicating the place in the music. The red line starts at the beginning when the song starts, and when a user pauses the song, the red line pauses, as does the song. Users also have the ability to combine music pieces, play music pieces simultaneously, and to add their own notes.

Shareity

Shareity is a MEAN stack (but don’t worry, it’s still nice) web application and it’s my current side project. The goal with Shareity is to create a charity based social media application that elevates the number of people giving to charities and volunteering. Users create a profile with wishes they have, like “I wish to alleviate child poverty” and then their dashboards will be filled with charities related to the wishes they have. Their friends would then be notified about their donations or volunteering.
