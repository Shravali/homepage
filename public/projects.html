SearchSmarter

SearchSmarter is a Chrome extension that can optimize users' Google search results. Users can then choose varies ways to optimize their search, such as searching only for scholarly articles or a particular file type, such as PowerPoints or PDFs. SearchSmarter is automatically enabled only when users are on the Google search page. Despite this, SearchSmarter doesn't have an obnoxious popup that detracts from users' googling experience. Instead, the extension, if hovered over, simply asks: "Are you unhappy with your search results?" Then users can start optimizing through the links and suggestions the extension provides. Get better search results when using Google search!
